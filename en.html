Goals

Don’t get blue

We’re KU Leuven now. That’s only the beginning.

It’s a lot harder to organize and finance activities at Group T or somewhere else. That’s why it’s very important for Industria to work together with Group T and the Student Council to make a great year of the upcoming year.

How? If we have a good communication with Group T and the Student Council and don’t neglect that we can prevent lots of problems.

And of course we still have to let our voices hear at the LOKO AV.

Course Service

We’re in Leuven for our studies. The Course Service is the most important function to provide the students with books at the right time.

We want to provide you with the right books at the right time so you can follow all your courses without any problem.

Members

Industria is a student organization. Meaning, doing activities for you, guys! We want you to enjoy some entertainment, some sport activities or cultural trips.

Still know some fun activities? Do you have ideas what every student wants to do? Feel free to let us know!

But don’t hesitate to help us if you want! Do you want to join the best group of builders at the 24h run? No problem! Do you want to organize a fun activity with us? No problem! It’s all a possibility!

We want to involve you in our organisation and plan future activities based on your opinion!

Business Relations

In these financial uncertain times it’s important that there enough funds to support the organisation so we can organize activities at the lowest prizes. But business relations is also important for the students to get a first contact with companies. We want to offer you workshops or interesting readings from people with experience. Those are only two examples of things BR can provide you.

Future

What’s going to happen with Industria in the future? What about our ‘fakbar’ Délibéré? What about the relation Industria-Student Council? How will life be at Group T within 10 years? Next year will be a very important year, to lay a foundation for our Industria successors and to assure they can still organize activities as Industria.
